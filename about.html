Our Journey

From a visionary partnership to a global leader in digital education

2018 - Foundation

HUAWEI and University of Dodoma establish strategic partnership to create Africa's first comprehensive digital learning ecosystem, focusing on ICT education and innovation.

2019 - Platform Launch

Launch of the first digital learning platform with AI-powered personalization, virtual labs, and industry-certified courses reaching 5,000 initial students.

2020 - Global Expansion

Rapid expansion during global digitalization, reaching 25,000 students across 15 African countries. Introduction of remote labs and cloud-native learning experiences.

2022 - Innovation Hub

Establishment of Africa's largest EdTech innovation hub, featuring 5G labs, AI research centers, and startup incubation programs.

2024 - Global Recognition

Recognized as UNESCO's flagship digital education program for Africa, serving 50,000+ students with 98% job placement rate for graduates.
