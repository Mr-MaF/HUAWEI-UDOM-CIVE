Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for courses?

You can apply through our online portal or contact our admissions team for personalized guidance.

What are the admission requirements?

Requirements vary by program. Generally, you need a high school diploma or equivalent and basic computer literacy.

Are there scholarship opportunities?

Yes, we offer various scholarship programs for exceptional students and those from underrepresented communities.

What support is available for students?

We provide 24/7 technical support, academic mentoring, career counseling, and peer learning communities.
